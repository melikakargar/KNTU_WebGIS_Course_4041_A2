How to Use

  1. Search: Enter a location in the search bar and click "Search"
  2. Get Weather: Click anywhere on the map to get weather data
  3. Current Location: Click "My Location" to center the map on your position
  4. Navigation: Use mouse scroll to zoom, drag to pan
